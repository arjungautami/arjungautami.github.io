Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where are my screenshots and recordings saved?
    All screenshots and screen recordings are stored locally on your device. You can access them in your Photos library or the folder selected in the app.

  • Does the app upload my recordings?
    No. All captured screenshots and recordings remain on your device. The app does not upload or share your data with external servers.

  • Can I choose where files are saved?
    Yes. The app allows you to select a folder on your device to organize and store your recordings and screenshots.

  • The recording feature is not working. What should I do?
    Make sure Screen Recording permission is enabled and that you have granted the required system permissions.
