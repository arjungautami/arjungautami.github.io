Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the CaptureKit application (“the App”) handles user information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

Overview

CaptureKit is a utility application that allows users to capture screenshots and record screen activity directly on their device. The app is designed to function without collecting or storing personal information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All screenshots and screen recordings created using the App remain stored locally on the user's device.

We do not track users, collect analytics, or gather usage data.

Permissions Used

To provide its core functionality, the App may request the following system permissions:

  • Screen Recording – Required to enable screen recording functionality.
  • Photos Library Access – Used to save captured screenshots or recordings to the user's photo library.

These permissions are used solely for providing the app’s functionality and are never used for data collection.

Data Storage

All media files generated using the App are stored locally on the user’s device. The App does not upload any content to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data collection services.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
